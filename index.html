Commission process
  • the amount of time I need to finish a drawing varies greatly and depends heavily on the time of the year; contact me for more information
  • you don't have to provide refrences but if you don't I will ask more questions about the design you have in mind
  • payment after approving the sketch
  • especially with complex drawings, even after the sketch is approved I might message you frequently to update you on the progress and ask if there are any changes to be made


Other
  • inform me if you do not wish for me to upload the commissioned work to my portfolio/social media
  • if you would like to commission me for a drawing that doesn't fall into any of the categories metioned above, feel free to contact me to discuss the details
  • I do not differentiate between busts, half-bodies and full-bodies; the more zoomed in the scene the more details will be included
  • I will send .psd (or .mdp) file or a timelapse of your commission upon request
  • furry and MLP welcome
  • detailed armor/mechanical parts welcome
  • I might decline to draw weird/controversial pictures :P
